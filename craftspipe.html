Pipe Cleaner Crafts

Use these craft ideas to work on building speech and language with your child. These crafts are wonderful for eliciting sounds and words as well as working on repetition. Please note some of the pages may take a few seconds to load. It's worth the wait! Resource information provided by artistshelpingchildren.org.

Kid's Pipe Cleaner Crafts - B

Bank Craft Project - This bank made out of a chip can, wrapping paper, and pipe cleaners makes a great craft project for kids. They can create it for themselves or as a gift for mom or dad.

Bendable Magnetic Animals and People - These bendable, fuzzy magnetic pipe cleaner animals and people are simple to make and great to play with later. They make wonderful refrigerator magnets that you can pose in incredible ways. If you make more than one, you can create an adorable group of critters.

Bookworm - This pipe cleaner bookworm is designed to encourage the littlest reader in your house to keep on reading! After making this new reading buddy your child can tell stories with him. They can be as personalized as you like.

Butterfly Wand - This is a great craft to do at a children's party. Before the party, glue all the pipe cleaners to popsicle sticks. The children can do everything else themselves during the party.

Button Flowers - Instructions on how to make really cute flowers with your children out of pipe cleaners and buttons.

Kid's Pipe Cleaner Crafts - C

Candy Cane Reindeer - Very simple and very inexpensive project for children. This craft is great for the winter holidays.

Caterpillar Keepsake Box - This craft is really great. I found it on the San Diego Zoo site and the craft is really awesome. When the craft is completed your child will be able to hide things inside of the caterpillar. I told you this craft was cool!

Charm Bracelet Craft - Here is a fun charm bracelet for kids to make. All you need is a pipe cleaner, a few charms, beads and miniature pink satin roses.

Chenille Candy Canes - This craft is so easy that it would be perfect for toddlers and preschoolers to do. Hang this beautiful candy canes from your Christmas Tree this year... your children will love it!

Coffee Filter Butterflies - Make this very cute and very easy craft with your child. Even the youngest preschooler will be able to fully enjoy this craft.

Kid's Pipe Cleaner Crafts - D

Just Ducky Duck - Cute and fuzzy, this pipe cleaner chick might be a duckling or, with another color scheme, a baby penguin or goose or swan.

Kid's Pipe Cleaner Crafts - E

Easy Stained Glass - This craft is so much fun to do with kids. Colored "glass" shapes can be used for decorations or can be strung together and hung in front of a window.

Egg Carton Animals - This is an easy way to make animals using egg cartons, crayons or markers, scissors, and pipe cleaners. Googly eyes are a nice touch. A cork (or similar object) is needed for the camel's head.

Egg Carton Critters - Check out these "Green Eggs and Ham" Activities by Seussville. This crafts are so cool and I am sure your kids will love them!

Egg Carton Spider - This is one spider that your child will not be afraid of. This spider is so much fun to make. This is a great rainy day craft.

Egg Carton Tulips - Make a bouquet of delicate eggshell tulips for Easter, using eggshells (or foam egg cartons sections) and pipe cleaners. Younger children will find it easier to use the egg carton sections.

Kid's Pipe Cleaner Crafts - H

Haunted Pencil Pot - This Haunted Pencil Pot can be created at a Halloween party as a craft activity or it can be a centerpiece for the table that your guest can take home with them.

Hershey's Kisses Kritters - Help your children make this fun craft project out of Hershy's Kisses and Pipe Cleaners. Not only are they yummy, but they are so much fun to make. Great for birthday parties.

Kid's Pipe Cleaner Crafts - M

May Basket - This is a great craft for children of all ages... even young children. This craft uses cups, pipe cleaners, and sequins to make a basket that actually hold items.

Mexican Paper Flower - These flowers are so beautiful and fun to make. Help your child make these flowers and spray a little perfume on them. Let your child hand them out to loved ones.

Monkeys - Turn pom-poms and pipe cleaners into silly-looking monkeys that you'll love to have hanging around.

Mother's Day Flower Pot - This craft is so amazing. This is the perfect craft for Dad's to do with their kids as a gift for their Mom for Mother's Day. There is a spot to insert a picture on the flower... so cute!

Kid's Pipe Cleaner Crafts - H

Photo Flower Arrangement - This craft is to be done as parent and child. It is not an easy craft for the child to do on his or her own. But, the craft is a great craft to do together. Craft involves pipe cleaners, photos, and other craft materials.

Pipe cleaner Characters - Help your kids make a whole family of these characters. You guys can make a Mommy, Daddy, Brother, Sister, baby, Grandmother, Grandfather, etc.

Pipe Cleaner Dinosaur - Make this extremely cute craft with your kids. This craft project is made from pipe cleaners and a pencil.

Pipe Cleaner People - Making pipe cleaner humans is a snap. Make a family of figures and dress them up any way you'd like.

Pipe Cleaner Sculptures - Bend and twist the pipe cleaners into shapes of people, animals, cars ... let your children use their imaginations with this craft.

Pipe Cleaner Teddies - Make these cute teddy bears with your kids out of pipe cleaners. Very easy and so much fun.

Kid's Pipe Cleaner Crafts - S

Santa's Reindeer - This is a really cute craft for Christmas. This is a great craft for toddlers and preschoolers. Help your child make the Reindeer and then let him or her hang the Reindeer proudly for all to see.

Shiny Wreaths - Make beautiful wreaths with your children for Christmas this year. Your children will be thrilled to help you make Christmas decorations for the house.

Sock Puppet - Instructions for two different ways to make a sock puppet. One way being a hand puppet and the other way being a stick puppet. Both ways use pipe cleaners.

Snowflakes - Grow your own snowflakes in a jar. Adult supervision is required, as you need boiling water. After you have made snow flakes you can experiment with other shapes, too.

Space Helmet - Your child will absolutely love this craft. The craft involves paper mache and pipe cleaners and the finished project is so cool!

Spider Buddies - This is a great craft to do with children of all ages. Make this fun craft out of Styrofoam balls and pipe cleaners.

Spooky Garland - This is a great Halloween project. Or, if your children are in to spooky things then it can be a year round craft. Make this great garland craft out of facial tissue, wiggly eyes, and pipe cleaners.

Styrofoam Bunny - This Easter Bunny will Hop! into your heart and your home for a short time only, until next year. Make a few to display around your home, give away and place on your table during dinner. Your children will enjoy making them to welcome the Easter Bunny. Make a day of Easter fun for them including this craft and others as well as an Easter Egg Hunt.

Kid's Pipe Cleaner Crafts - W

World's Greatest Mom Magnet - This is an amazing craft for Dad's to do with their children. This pipe cleaner craft is a great finished project to give to their Moms as a gift.
